Acquiring diamonds. It is a very sensitive task, but the acquisition bears virtue and substance. Jonah Levi delivers exquisite natural diamonds to make every single of these significant moments worthwhile. As the stone is set into the jewelry, it marks the beginning of a venture. The sparkle you see, the excellence you immediately comprehend, and the affirmative smile your customers give—it all becomes a pleasurable ritual.

Who we are

Jonah Levi is a diamond wholesaler headquartered in the South of Germany, and is active in distributing exquisite, polished, natural diamonds to demanding institutional clients, such as jewelery manufacturers, watch manufacturers, and independent jewelery designers.

What we do

We service our clients with a rich assortment of the finest, loose, GIA-certified diamonds, ranging from .3 points up to 5 carats. We also offer high-quality non-certified diamonds in the range of .01 points up to 3 carats—always yielding best value for our clients due to direct contact to the bourses.

Where we are going

Our direct and established contacts at the international diamond bourses, allow us to provide exceptional prices and long term supply arrangements for key accounts. On these grounds, we will continue to expand our operations internationally.
